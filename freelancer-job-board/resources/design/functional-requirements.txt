Functional Requirements (MVP User Stories)

1. As a freelancer, I can apply to a job that a client has posted.
2. As a freelancer, I can view my submitted applications and their status.
3. As a client, I can post a job with a title, description, and category.
4. As a client, I can view a list of jobs I have posted.
5. As an authenticated user, I can log in and be shown role-specific menu options.
